Amy Adams

With her ivory complexion and flaming red hair, it would be hard for Amy Adams to look anything but striking. On the red carpet, her choices are classic Hollywood glamour, timeless and elegant: frothy floor-sweeping gowns in soft pastels from Oscar de la Renta, Marchesa and Elie Saab, and hourglass-silhouetted jewel tones from Giambattista Valli and Roland Mouret.
